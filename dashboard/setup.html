✨ Setup in 5 Easy Steps

Takes only 5 minutes! Follow these simple steps:

1

Create Your Telegram Bot

  • Open Telegram app
  • Search for @BotFather
  • Send: /newbot
  • Follow prompts and copy your bot token
  • Start your bot and get your Chat ID from @userinfobot
📹 Watch Tutorial
2

Fork This Repository

  • Click the "Fork" button on GitHub
  • This creates your own copy of the project
  • Free and takes 10 seconds!
🔗 Go to GitHub Repo
3

Add Your Secrets

  • In your forked repo: Settings → Secrets → Actions
  • Add TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN (from Step 1)
  • Add TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID (from Step 1)
📖 Detailed Guide
4

Configure Your Preferences

  • Edit data/user_config.json in your repo
  • Set your favorite team (CSK, MI, RCB, etc.)
  • Choose city and max ticket price
⚙️ Generate Config
5

Enable GitHub Actions

  • Go to Actions tab in your repo
  • Click "I understand my workflows, go ahead and enable them"
  • Done! 🎉 You'll start receiving alerts within 15 minutes
✅ Final Step
